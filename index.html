Our Programs

We offer comprehensive educational programs designed to meet the needs of students at every stage of their development.

Preschool

Our preschool program focuses on early childhood development through play-based learning, fostering curiosity and social skills.

  • Age-appropriate curriculum
  • Social-emotional development
  • Early literacy & numeracy

Elementary

Our elementary program builds strong academic foundations while encouraging creativity and independent thinking.

  • Core subject mastery
  • Project-based learning
  • Character development

Junior High School

Our junior high program prepares students for senior high with rigorous academics and leadership opportunities.

  • Advanced coursework
  • STEM & arts integration
  • College preparation

MAKWILLS Pediatric Therapy Center

Specialized services to support children with unique learning needs, including Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA), Speech & Language Therapy, and Special Education (SPED).
